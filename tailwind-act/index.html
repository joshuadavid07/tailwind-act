About

Hi! My nickname is Josh.

But you can call me anything you like! I move through the world with a quiet kind of intensity — someone who finds meaning in the details and takes things one step at a time.

My days are built around learning things that feel just out of reach. Web development, visual design, the craft of making something useful and considered. I care less about the output and more about the discipline behind it.

Outside of screens, I am drawn to stillness — long films, slow food, and places that earn their silence. I am building toward a life that is small in footprint but dense in meaning.

Aspirations

Goals

Near-term

Build something real

A project that exists beyond the classroom. Something with a purpose, an audience, and the kind of attention to detail that makes someone stop and look twice.

Near-term

Master the fundamentals

JavaScript, responsive layout, accessibility. The unglamorous groundwork that makes everything else possible. No shortcuts.

Long-term

Work from anywhere

A laptop, a reliable connection, and the freedom to choose the view outside the window. Not a fantasy — a plan with steps.

Long-term

Live abroad for a year

Japan or Canada. Long enough to become a temporary local. To learn a place by its rhythms rather than its landmarks.

Ongoing

Keep the list growing

See the northern lights. Finish a 30-day challenge. Attend a festival far from home. The point is not to complete the list — the point is to never stop adding to it.
